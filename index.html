Instagram Growth Strategy

+300% Engagement

The Challenge

Client A had an Instagram page but no clear direction. Inconsistent posting, no defined brand voice, and little to no engagement from their audience.

Our Approach

We started from scratch. Our team audited their existing content, identified what their audience actually responds to, and built a full content strategy around it. We defined their brand voice, designed a consistent visual identity, and created a monthly content calendar that took the guesswork out of posting.

The Outcome

Within 60 days, their Instagram engagement tripled. More importantly, they started receiving inbound enquiries directly through their platform.

Local Café Digital Transformation

Zero to Digital Presence

The Challenge

A cozy local café had been running for over a year with loyal walk-in customers but almost no digital presence. No strategy, no consistent content, and no way for new customers to discover them online. Great food, zero visibility.

Our Approach

We stepped in and built their entire online presence from scratch. We created a content strategy centered around their food, ambiance, and story — the things that make people want to visit. Reels, aesthetic food photography direction, customer testimonials, and strategic hashtag use were all part of the plan. We also optimized their Google My Business profile to drive local discovery.

The Outcome

Good food deserves a good audience. We helped them find theirs — transforming their digital presence into a customer acquisition channel that drove foot traffic and built their brand locally.
